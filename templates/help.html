How to Use This App

Welcome to your Homework Manager! Here’s how everything works:

  • Add Assignments: Click Add to create a new homework entry with title, course, due date, and notes.
  • Edit Assignments: Select Edit on any card to update its details.
  • Mark as Done: Check the box next to an assignment title to mark it complete.
  • Delete: Click 🗑️ on a card to open a confirmation popup — then confirm deletion.
  • Search: Use the 🔍 icon to filter and find assignments by keywords or course.
  • Logout: Ends your session securely.
